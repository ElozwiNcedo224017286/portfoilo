🌸 Hair Care Tips (for lasting luxury)

  • ✨ Use sulfate-free shampoo & cold water rinses to preserve shine.
  • ✨ Detangle with a wide-tooth comb starting from ends.
  • ✨ Apply argan oil 2x per week to keep strands moisturized.
  • ✨ Store wigs on a mannequin or silk bag to maintain shape.
  • ✨ Avoid heat above 180°C (356°F) on raw hair textures.
